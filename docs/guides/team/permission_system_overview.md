# Permission system overview

RAGFlow manages permissions through teams, sharing scopes, and resource operation permissions. These controls determine who can see a resource and what actions they can perform on it.

## Team member management

Team member management controls who can join a team and participate in team-level collaboration.

Team owners can invite users, remove members, and view joined teams from the **Team** page.

## Resource sharing scope

The resource sharing scope determines whether a resource is visible only to its owner or shared with team members or collaborators.

In the open-source edition, resources can be shared with team members. In RAGFlow Enterprise, sharing scopes can be configured with finer-grained organization and collaboration controls.

## Resource operation permissions

Resource operation permissions define what users can do after a resource becomes visible to them.

Permissions can apply to knowledge bases, chat applications, Agents, MCP resources, memories, Search applications, and models.
